Templates

Full fill-in templates are in the course materials: see the printed/LMS copy of “Deliverables & Templates”. Quick view of the two most important:

Traceability Matrix (D5.1) — the Golden Thread

One row per main feature:

Problem / Pain Requirement (FR) Solution / Design Feature built Test
students forget deadlines FR-3 remind 1 day before scheduler + Task entity reminder feature test_reminds_1_day_before

Self-report + AI Log (D7) — write this yourself

Keep it 1–2 pages. Must be personal and different from teammates:

  1. What I personally did — my specific contributions
  2. What I learned
  3. My AI Log — task · tool · what AI gave · what I changed · why (explain ≥1 part in your own words)
  4. What I got out of it / would do differently
  5. Sign: "This report is my own work; AI use is logged honestly."

Submission rules

  • Deadlines are the start of class that week unless announced.
  • On time = full credit · late = half · missing = 0 (for the attendance/submission score).
  • Every milestone needs an honest AI Usage Log — missing it caps that milestone at 50%.
