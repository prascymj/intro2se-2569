What this course is really about

AI can already write code. So this course is about what makes you the engineer:

framing the right problem · making decisions · checking what's built · being accountable · working in a team

You will go through the whole software process once, as a team — requirements → design → build → test → secure → present — using AI as a helper that you direct and verify.

The one rule that matters most 🔑

You may use AI. But for everything you hand in you must:

  1. Log it — what you asked AI, what it gave you
  2. Verify it — check and fix what it produced
  3. Explain it — out loud, in the oral exam

Work you cannot explain earns no credit. "AI wrote it and I just submitted it" will not pass.
